Premier 3M Distributor

FayJan is a premier 3M industrial distributor serving the abrasive, adhesive, tape, and safety markets across Pennsylvania.


With locations in Montgomery and York, PA, we provide same-state delivery and on-site service programs that keep your production line moving — no waiting on national distributors.


Whether you need a single specialized tool or a fully managed inventory program, our team brings the expertise to get you exactly what you need — when you need it.
